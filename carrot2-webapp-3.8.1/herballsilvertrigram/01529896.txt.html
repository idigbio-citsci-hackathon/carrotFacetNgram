PUERTO RICO
Sinocalamus oldhamii (Munro) McClure
Cultivated at Federal Experiment Station, Paris Field, MayagUez. Culms 10 m tall, 6" in diam., placed in a discrete clump, very erect. Young culms glaucous, bluish-green (especially where covered by sheaths).
Sheaths brown, early deciduous, its blade erect, appressed to culm. Culms branching only at uppermost nodes. Beautiful foliage; blades shining green, more or less coriaceous, One of the most attractive bamboos seen.
Many new culms now. Most leaves diseased.
