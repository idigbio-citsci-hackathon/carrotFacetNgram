Oenothera rosea Soland. det. C. D. Adams 1963
St. Andrew Parish: Grassy hillside just below Mt. Horeb, c. 3900 feet. Fairly common.
Hypanthium and calyx lobes greenish-white, tinged with reddish-purple; petals bright reddish-purple, greenish yellow at bases; anthers brown, style and stigmas white, stigmas tipped with reddish purple; ovary m. r. crosby green, ribbed with red.
